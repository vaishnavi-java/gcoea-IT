  It is my pleasure to welcome you to Government College of Engineering, Amravati, an Autonomous Institute of Government of Maharashtra. Government College of Engineering, Amravati was established at 1964. Government College of Engineering, Amravati as set itself a vision “Towards Global Technological Excellence”. The Campus has highly equipped infrastructure, which fascilitate a positive and truthful learning environment for students. Government College of Engineering, Amravati offers seven Under Graduate Programs and six Post Graduate Programs along with full time Ph.D. Programs in various disciplines. It houses more than 2250 plus students and 73 faculties. The academic results of Government College of Engineering, Amravati are the best among all colleges affiliated to Sant Gadge Baba Amravati University, Amravati. Government College of Engineering, Amravati provides a students centered learning environment with close students faculty interaction.

           The academic activities concentrate on helping the students to gain an excellent knowledge based and in the development of skills to implement them. The special emphasis is on Outcome Based Education (OBE).

           The Placement record is excellent with highest annual package of 4.5 lacs. We are one of the premier institute of Vidarbha Region, digital nurtured inclusive by Cognizant. Our students to get placed in many reputed multinational companies across India & abroad.

           The Library houses equipped with International Journals along with latest books of respective disciplines and is open for extended hours to the students.

            The college is committed to provide continuous support and encouragement for the students having innovative ideas, now students are getting awards, prize, money in this regard.

            The institute has grown over the years in pursuit of this vision and cross several mile stones of excellence. This has become possible only due to the tireless efforts of its faculties and staff members and worthy contribution of its dedicated students and alumni. I extend my best wishes to all students in their chosen career path.
